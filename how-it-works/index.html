Overview

OneRoom gathers curious people from all over the world to learn together in small, short, highly interactive classes.

  • Engage with great teachers in a small class
  • Talk face-to-face with other's who share your interests
  • Connect with like-minded people, no matter where you happen to be
  • Get feedback on your creative work

We're here to help you start your project, hone your craft, and build your community.

It's like a conversation around a table...without the table. But don't take our word for it! It's like this:

Interact with real, live humans through your webcam

Meet people who nerd-out about the same topics as you

Bite-sized classes that fit your schedule

What is the classroom like?

The rooms are like classrooms with movable chairs. Different "modes" support different kinds of interaction.

Discuss

Engage in small, live discussions with everyone. Be seen, be heard, be inspired.

Workshop

Focus on student work or inspirational materials for discussion.

Breakout

Get to know the other participants in breakout sessions.

Share

Share your work and get candid feedback.

What is a class like?

Classes are 20 or 40 minutes long and led by knowledgeable instructors. Students can join from anywhere in the world.

How is OneRoom different?

It's the best of taking classes in person with the convenience of online.

Will it work for my schedule?

Probably! When you sign up for a class, we'll ask for your scheduling preferences, and do our best to accommodate. New classes are being scheduled all the time.

How much does it cost?

Prices for multi-session classes vary; 20 minute "micro" classes are $5.

Is it safe for introverts?

Yes! It's ok to be quiet. Active listening is also encouraged.

Will I be in over my head?

Most classes are open to all, so assume that includes you, no matter your level of experience (unless the class description or title indicate otherwise - "advanced workshop" or the like). In short, beginners are welcome.

Can I teach?

Please contact us if you want to teach. We'd love to talk.
