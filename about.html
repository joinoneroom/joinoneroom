Our Story

Scott Forman and Jeff Kolesky founded OneRoom in the summer of 2014. They were inspired by their own experiences with online courses to build a new way to learn online that didn't feel so lonely and isolating, and that emphasizes discussion and the social experience of learning.

We're based in San Francisco, and are part of the Innerspace community of startups.
