What is this thing?

Great question.

OneRoom is a new kind of online writing class.

OneRoom creative writing classes feel like live workshops.

  • Talk face-to-face with other writers
  • Engage with great teachers in a small class
  • Receive in-person and written feedback on your work
  • Connect with writers from anywhere in the world

OneRoom is a clean, friendly interface focused on writing and community.

Be notified of upcoming classes!

The Experience

Discuss

Engage in small, live discussions with everyone. Be seen, be heard, be inspired.

Workshop

Focus on readings or student work for discussion.

Share

Share readings and work easily.

Comment

Compose private notes and share comments on one another's work.
