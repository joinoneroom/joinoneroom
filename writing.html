What's this all about?

OneRoom gathers writers from all over the world to collaborate, learn, and write together.

  • Engage with great teachers in a small class
  • Talk face-to-face with other writers
  • Connect with writers, no matter where you happen to be
  • Receive in-person and written feedback on your work

We're here to help you start your project, hone your craft, and build your writing community.

How does it work?

The rooms are like classrooms with movable chairs.

Different "modes" support different kinds of interaction.

Discuss

Engage in small, live discussions with everyone. Be seen, be heard, be inspired.

Workshop

Focus on readings or student work for discussion.

Breakout

Get to know the other participants in breakout sessions.

Share

Share your work and get candid feedback.

Will it work for my schedule?

Probably! Most classes are offered in the evenings Pacific or Eastern, and on weekends. We also have microclasses, which are exactly what they sound like. If you don't see a class that works for your schedule, weâ€™re adding new ones all the time.

How much does it cost?

Class prices vary according to the experience of the teacher, but on average they cost about $30 per hour of class time. The price for microclasses is as little as $10.

Is it safe for introverts?

Yes! It's ok to be quiet. Active listening is also encouraged.

Will I be in over my head?

Most classes are open to all, so assume that includes you, no matter your level of experience (unless the class description or title indicate otherwise - "advanced workshop" or the like). In short, beginners are welcome.

Can I teach?

Please contact us if you want to teach. We'd love to talk.
