The Teaching Experience

OneRoom handles all the nuances of the workshop format. Assign readings, invite students to comment on them, select students to be workshopped, manage other students' written responses. And in live meetings, mingle before the session starts and after it ends, and manage the stage just like you would in person, directing attention and steering the conversation. In an easy-to-use, friendly-feeling browser-based classroom.

Workshop

Bring readings or a student's work into focus for discussion.

Share

Share readings easily, and allow students to share their work securely.

Comment

Students can make private notes and share comments on one another's work.

Discuss

Facilitate live discussions with everyone on an equal footing.

Mingle

Have multiple parallel conversations in informal "mingling" mode.

Breakout

Break into smaller groups for planned or impromptu exercises.
